How Maintenance Mode Works
When you send a maintenance announcement, a full-screen blue glassy popup instantly appears on every user's device — there is no close button. Users cannot use the app until you click Clear Now or delete the record from history. Set a duration to auto-clear after a set time, or leave at 0 to keep it until manually removed.
