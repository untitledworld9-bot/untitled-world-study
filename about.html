About Untitled World

Untitled World is a distraction-free study platform designed for students preparing for JEE, NEET and board exams. Our goal is to make studying simple, focused and effective.

Here you can track your progress, use study timers, create playlists from YouTube lectures and soon take mock tests — all in one place.

This platform is built to help students stay consistent, motivated and productive.
